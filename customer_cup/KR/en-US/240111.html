LG gram Link Consent to Collect and Use Personal Information

 

LG Electronics (hereinafter referred to as “Company") complies with personal information protection provisions of the relevant laws such as Act on Promotion of Information and Communication Network Utilization and Information Protection and Personal Information Protection Act, and collects and uses personal information of User who uses the LG gram Link service. Check the related details in Privacy Policy posted in the Company’s SW solution.

 

1. Collected Personal Information Items and Collection Methods

 

① Items of Personal Information Collected

 

“Company” collects the following personal information regarding the LG gram Link service (hereinafter referred to as “service”).

A.    Required items

- Push token (information collected upon agreeing to receive the app push)

- Log information (access and use logs)

 

※ Some information may be collected selectively upon using certain services.

※ When collecting personal information for a short term for a special purpose, a separate notification is provided and User's consent is acquired before collection.

 

② Method of collecting personal information

- User’s personal information, when User connects to the mobile application with the Internet connection device in the process of use, automatically collects the personal information related to the device.

- Personal information, such as device use information, is collected automatically through the smart device, website, or log analysis program according to the app use.

 

2. Purpose of Collection of Personal Information

 

Company uses collected personal information for the following purposes.

 

● Services provided: Disable mobile device connection, share photos and images, synchronize photos and images, categorize photos in the PC automatically, Clipboard, mirroring, expand the screen, share the keyboard and mouse, update device connection information

 

3. Periods of Retention and Use of Personal Information

 

In principle, User’s personal information is used and retained during the service use period. Also, Company can retain personal information during the period specified in the regulations of relevant laws.

 

4. Notification of Disadvantages in Non-consent or Non-acceptance of Agreement

You have rights to reject the consent to collect and use the information above. If you do not agree, however, you may be restricted from the service use.
