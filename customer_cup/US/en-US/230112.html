A. Required collection items

3. Periods of Retention and Use of Personal Information

 

In principle, User’s personal information is used and retained during the service use period. Also, “Company” can retain personal information during the period specified in the regulations of relevant laws.

 

Classification: Personal information included in commercial books and important documents on business

Retention period: 10 years

Applicable law: Commercial Law

 

Classification: Email address, etc. that is necessary for user confirmation during provision of data for communication verification

Retention period: 12 months

Applicable law: Protection of Communications Secrets Act

 

Classification: Date and time of telecommunication, start and end time of telecommunication, subscription number such as sending/receiving communication number, number of use, location tracking information used by base station for confirming location of telecommunication device connected to information communication network, etc.

Retention period: 12 months

Applicable law: Protection of Communications Secrets Act

 

Classification: Log data of computer communication and the Internet related to User’s use of telecommunication via computer or the Internet, tracking data for locating information communication device used by User to connect to information communication network via computer communication or the Internet

Retention period: 3 months

Applicable law: Protection of Communications Secrets Act

 

Classification: Customer consultation records

Retention period: Final end time of A/S task according to customer consultation or 30 days after A/S task

 

Classification: If a dispute arises between “Company” and User, such as a civil complaint or lawsuit, which cannot be resolved within the Service period

Retention period: Up to the point when the dispute is resolved
