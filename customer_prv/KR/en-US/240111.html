11. Technical and Administrative Safeguards for Protection of Personal Information

Company has established the following technical and administrative measures to prevent loss, theft, leakage, alteration, or damage of personal information of Users in processing them.


① Technical Protection Measures

- Personal information of User is protected by a password which is managed by a security device (SSL) that can securely transmit personal information in the network using encryption algorithms.

- In order to protect against external intrusions such as hacking, each server controls unauthorized access from outside by using an intrusion prevention system as well as a vulnerability analysis system, and is also equipped with capable technical tools which guarantee security in the system for full commitment to security in handling personal information.

- Company stores information for identification such as password and biological data through unilateral encryption.

- In order to respond to personal information infringement, Company stores access records for the personal information processing system and takes measures to prevent forgery and alteration, such as backup.

- When the personal information processing system outputs personal information, the purpose of the output is specified (printing, screen display, file generation, etc.), and the output items are minimized according to the purpose.


② Administrative measures for protection - Access to User’s personal information is restricted to a minimum number of persons.

- If a manager of the personal information is changed due to a personnel shift such as by transfer or retirement, the applicable access right to the personal information processing system is transferred or canceled without delay. In such a case, the history of grants, changes, and cancellations of the access right is recorded then kept for at least [ 5 ] years.

- The personal information processing system and the PC used by the handler of the personal information are set in a way that the personal information being processed cannot be disclosed to unauthorized parties through Internet homepages, P2P, or sharing settings.

- In accordance with the standards recommended by Act on Collection, Use, and Destruction of Personal Information (related law to regulations such as Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, etc.), backgrounds and proofs of collection purposes, use periods, used items, destruction, etc. of the information shall be provided, and internal policies and processes have been stipulated in the regard.

- Regular in-house trainings and outsourced trainings, such as on acquiring new security techniques and on obligations of protecting personal information, are conducted for the employees who process personal information.

- Internal procedures are set in place which prevent information leakage through humans by the means of all employees’ security pledge and monitor fulfillment of and compliance with the personal information protection policy by all employees.

- The handover and takeover of personal information handler's duties are carried out under strictly maintained security and the information related to liabilities for personal information accidents after entering and leaving Company are clearly delivered.

- Company shall not be liable for any troubling issues arising from User’s personal mistakes or intrinsic dangers of the Internet. User shall properly manage his or her own ID and password to protect the personal information and shall be responsible in management.

- In other cases, if the loss, leakage, alteration, or damage of personal information occurs due to the internal manager’s mistake or accident in technical management, it will be immediately notified to User and seek appropriate measures and compensations.
